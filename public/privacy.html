Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the "EML to PDF" Chrome extension ("the Extension") collects, how it is used, and what stays entirely on your device.

Summary

  • Your email files and their content never leave your device. Parsing .eml files and generating PDFs happens entirely locally, inside your browser.
  • The only information that reaches our server is what's needed to (a) verify your Google sign-in and (b) check or grant paid access. Neither of these ever includes your email content.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

What the Extension processes locally (never transmitted)

  • The content of any .eml file you open or convert, including sender/recipient details, message body, and attachments
  • The PDF files generated from your .eml files

None of the above is uploaded, transmitted, logged, or stored anywhere outside your own device. The Extension's core conversion functionality requires no internet connection.

What is sent to our server, and why

DataWhen it's sentPurpose
Your Google account email addressWhen you sign in, and when checking/restoring paid accessTo identify your account for unlocking paid features and to prevent unauthorized use
Purchase/payment confirmation (via our payment processor's webhook)When you complete a paymentTo grant your account extended access

We do not receive your Google password, contacts, or any other Google account data beyond the email address associated with your account, and only with your explicit sign-in action.

Third-party services

  • Google Sign-In — used to verify your identity. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Dodo Payments — our payment processor, used to handle purchases. Dodo Payments processes your payment details directly; we never see or store your card information. See Dodo Payments' Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

Data storage and retention

We store, on our server:

  • Your email address (if you've signed in)
  • Your account's access status
  • A record of any purchase transaction (for support and refund purposes)

We do not store your .eml files, their content, or any generated PDFs — these never reach our server in the first place.

You may request deletion of your stored account data at any time by contacting [SUPPORT EMAIL].

Permissions the Extension requests, and why

  • downloads — to save the generated PDF files to your device
  • storage — to remember your local preferences and usage locally in your browser
  • identity — to enable Google Sign-In
  • Host permission for our API domain — to communicate with our server for sign-in and purchase verification only, as described above

Children's privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
